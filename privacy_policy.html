Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2025

Chatty Cathy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you submit an application for freelance opportunities with us.

1. Information We Collect
Personal Information: Including your name, email address, phone number, location, LinkedIn profile, GitHub/Portfolio links.
Technical Skills: Details about your programming languages, frameworks, databases, version control systems, and other relevant technologies.
Professional Experience: Information about your development experience, freelance projects, notable clients, and availability.
Project Preferences: Your preferred project types and technologies.
Rates and Payment: Desired hourly rates and payment terms.
Additional Information: Cover letters, references, availability to start, and any other relevant details.
Attachments: Resume/CV and portfolio samples.

2. How We Use Your Information
Evaluation: To assess your qualifications and suitability for freelance projects.
Communication: To contact you regarding your application and potential opportunities.
Record-Keeping: To maintain records of applications for future reference.

3. Disclosure of Your Information
Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Data Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention
We will retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us at hr@chattycathy.com to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
